Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Yes. However, if you're making money directly or indirectly with LCBO API, be cool and send me a little bit of that sugar. I love working on LCBO API, but it takes a lot of my time and costs roughly $80/month to host. Donations help keep the wind in my sails and give me the freedom to make it even better.

I'm also open to sponsored feature development, send me an email if that's something you or your company is interested in.

Where does the data come from?

From the LCBO of course! LCBO API periodically crawls their website to get up-to-date information. Search engines like Google and Bing use this same technique to index web pages.

Can you add UPC information to products, please?

I do not currently have access to barcode information for all products that the LCBO stocks. I understand how useful barcode data would be for products and if this ever changes it will certainly be announced on @lcboapi's Twitter account.
